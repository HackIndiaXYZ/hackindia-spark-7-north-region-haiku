Her body has 4 seasons 🌸
Every month, she lives through four completely different versions of herself. Tap each to learn what happens.
🩸 Menstrual
Days 1–5
▼
Her hormones are at their lowest. The body is doing serious internal work — shedding, renewing, resting. Fatigue and cramps are real and valid, not dramatic.
🛌 Rest phase
🌱 Follicular
Days 6–13
▼
Estrogen rises. Energy comes back, creativity sharpens, she becomes more social and curious. This is the best time for plans, new things, and connection.
⚡ Energy phase
✨ Ovulatory
Days 14–16
▼
Estrogen and testosterone both peak. She is at her most confident, magnetic, and articulate. This 3-day window is her biological peak.
🌟 Peak phase
🌕 Luteal
Days 17–28
▼
Progesterone rises, serotonin drops. She slows down, feels more deeply, and becomes more sensitive. Irritability and anxiety are hormonal — not character flaws.
🌊 Deep phase
